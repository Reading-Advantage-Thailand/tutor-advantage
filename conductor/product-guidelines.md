# Product Guidelines

Date: 2026-02-23

## Product Tone

- Practical, transparent, and trust-first.
- Explicitly explain pricing, enrollment, and commission mechanics.
- Avoid hype language around MLM; emphasize educational outcomes and audited payout rules.

## UX Guidelines

- Mobile-first workflows for tutors and students.
- Keep enrollment flow short: class link -> account/login -> payment -> enrolled.
- Finance and payout views prioritize traceability over visual complexity.
- Show exact statuses for every critical event: payment, enrollment, settlement, payout.

## Policy Communication

- Always show consent boundaries before opening communication channels.
- Make guardian flows explicit and unavoidable for underage users.
- Show refund and clawback effects clearly in tutor earnings history.

## Educational Growth and Training Culture

- Emphasize student growth via program-wide benchmark assessments.
- Align tutor incentives with educational outcomes through badges and growth-based bonuses.
- Foster the "10% Training Time" culture by providing Uplines with visibility into Trainee performance and student growth metrics.
- Keep training and checklists integrated into the app to set expectations and professionalize the network.

## Content Guidelines

- Preserve workbook and article URL compatibility for legacy QR users.
- Keep level/book naming exact to printed materials.
- Display class package scope clearly as one-book package in V1.

## Data and Audit Guidelines

- Every monetary action must be reconstructable from immutable records.
- Separate attribution data from payout data.
- Never retroactively rewrite settled payout lineage.
